The Story

How I got here

I fell into design at sixteen when I tried to make my first blog look less terrible. That rabbit hole led me through graphic design, UI/UX, and eventually into code — because I wanted complete control over what I was building, from the first sketch to the final deploy.

Over the past eight years, I've designed and shipped products for startups, Fortune 500 companies, and everything in between. I've led design teams, built design systems from scratch, and spent more late nights debugging CSS than I'd care to admit. I believe the best products come from the people who can both imagine and execute.

When I'm not pushing pixels or writing code, you'll find me sketching typefaces, hiking trails near Manila, or obsessing over coffee extraction ratios. I'm currently exploring the intersection of AI and design tooling — the future of how we create is being rewritten, and I want to be part of that story.

"I don't believe in design for design's sake. I believe in design that solves, design that delights, design that respects every second of a user's time."
