About Me

Designer, developer, and
relentless problem solver.

Based in Brooklyn, NY — working at the intersection of design and engineering since 2018.

PR

I'm Patrick Reyes, a full-stack product designer who believes great software starts with deep empathy. I've spent the last eight years collaborating with startups, agencies, and Fortune 500 teams to ship products people genuinely love using.

My journey started with a psychology degree and a curiosity about why people abandon forms. That led me into UX research, then UI design, and eventually front-end engineering — because I kept wanting to be closer to the final product.

Today I lead design-to-code workflows end-to-end: from discovery interviews and wireframes through to production React components and design-system tokens. I believe the best designers write code, and the best engineers understand people.

When I'm not pushing pixels, you'll find me teaching a weekend UX workshop at the local library, tinkering with generative art, or hunting for the best cold brew in New York.
